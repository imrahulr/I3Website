Description

Most of us have lots of ideas but only few of them are able to successfully execute that one idea that makes all the difference.If you are fortunate to have found that killer idea, Maker's Square is your golden ticket. Maker's Square is a national event to put up your projects and exhibits ranging from civil, mechanical to robotics, chemical, physics experiments etc.This is your chance to make a difference to the world and get into the lime-light.
